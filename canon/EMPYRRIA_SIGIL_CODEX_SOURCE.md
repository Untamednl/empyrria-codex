Category: State Sigil

Associated Terms: Zenga, Zellia

### IAH-VEA

Category: Doctrine Sigil
Status: Approved
Meaning / Function: Inscription of declared structure—not felt, not trained. States what doctrine has already fixed as form—open, stable, and formally witnessed. Carries no implication of attainment, rehearsal, or affective cultivation.
Associated Terms: IAH-VEA
Geometry Logic: Vertical pillar read as primary bearer of declared order; doctrinal spacing and inscriptional rhythm (steady interval, ruled bands); frontal seal- or tablet-surface posture—declarative layout, shallow subordinate top lintel only (thin inset stroke; not a full fascia or baseline), minimal lateral flourish.
Color Logic: Stone, ash, inscribed metal on neutral ground; restrained contrast; archival, not ceremonial saturation.
Usage Context: Publication and fixation of doctrine—marking what is formally held true and witnessing declared order. Operates as record and designation; not channel, enhancement, nor force projection.
